The talk “Around and Around” on how to find artistic inspirations and how to design and create contemporary jewelry with strong brand identity

When: 16 August 2016 | 19.00 – 20.30

Where: Auditorium, TCDC

Find the inspiration in creating work piece and learn the art of contemporary jewelry design with Jiro Kamata, an international contemporary jewelry designer and artist. Jiro is going to introduce his background, his work, his thinking, designing and working process, his time at the Academy of Fine Arts Munich, his contemporary Jewelry exhibitions, his source of Inspirations, the Journey, lights, reflections and the sun.
